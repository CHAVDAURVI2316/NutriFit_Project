Privacy Policy

Why does NutriFit need health and lifestyle information ?

Health and lifestyle information is used only to understand your nutrition needs. It allows our nutritionists to provide safe, customized diet recommendations and lifestyle guidance. This data is never used for marketing without your consent.

What types of personal information does NutriFit collect ?

NutriFit is a nutrition-focused platform. Health and lifestyle data helps:

  • Understand your body needs
  • Create safe and personalized diet plans
  • Create safe and personalized diet plans
  • Avoid unsuitable food recommendations
  • Track progress and improvements
    • Without this data, accurate nutrition guidance would not be possible

    How are nutritionist payments and earnings data handled ?

    Payment-related information is handled securely through trusted payment gateways. NutriFit does not store sensitive payment details such as card numbers.

    What information is shared during a nutrition consultation ?

    During a consultation, users may share:

  • Health details (age, weight, height, medical history if any)
  • Diet habits and lifestyle information
  • Fitness and nutrition goals
    • This information is shared only with the assigned nutritionist to provide proper guidance

    What information is collected when I book an appointment ?

    When you book an appointment on NutriFit, we collect details such as your name, contact information, selected nutritionist, appointment date and time, and basic health information required for consultation.

    How can nutrition help with hormonal imbalances?

    Nutrition supports hormone balance by providing essential nutrients, stabilizing blood sugar, and improving metabolism. Proper meal timing and balanced macronutrients help manage symptoms like fatigue, weight changes, and mood swings. NutriFit creates personalized plans that work with your lifestyle to support thyroid health, PCOS, and metabolic issues naturally.

    Can nutrition replace medications for endocrine disorders ?

    No. Nutrition complements medications, helping them work more effectively. Our plans provide nutrient-rich meals that support hormone regulation, reduce inflammation, and improve energy while following your doctor’s guidance.

    How do children benefit from NutriFit plans?

    NutriFit’s personalized plans support a child’s healthy growth, immunity, and energy levels. Meals are designed to provide all essential nutrients for physical and cognitive development. Plans are age-appropriate, tasty, and easy to follow, helping children develop healthy eating habits from an early age. Parents receive guidance to make nutrition practical and stress-free at home, ensuring long-term wellness.

    How can I manage blood sugar with diet?

    You can manage blood sugar by eating balanced meals with low-glycemic foods, adequate protein, fiber, and healthy fats. Proper meal timing and portion control help prevent spikes and crashes. NutriFit’s personalized plans are tailored to your lifestyle, preferences, and medications, making it easier to follow consistently. With practical tips, healthy swaps, and regular guidance, you can maintain stable blood sugar and improve overall energy naturally.
